My story.

During high school, I attended a tech-focused institution that offered courses in graphic and web design. I found myself drawn to this field, and it set me on the path towards my eventual career.

After graduation, I pursued a degree in Musical Theatre while working retail to pay my way. However, when the pandemic struck, I shifted my focus towards the e-commerce branch of the store where I worked. My work there led to me becoming the community manager for both brands of the company, where I posted pictures, edited videos, and made reels for social media and in-store screens.

In 2021, I left that job to dedicate my time to completing my Musical Theatre degree. However, my passion for technology led me to enroll in the Foundations of Software Development program at Holberton School in early 2022. I was attracted to this program because it values both technical skills and soft skills, including public speaking, teamwork, and networking.

After completing the first trimester, I began working in the gaming industry as a QA tester. I have always had a passion for gaming, and working on some truly amazing games has been an incredible experience.

For my final project at Holberton, I worked as a Front-End Developer and UI/UX Designer, creating an app called "Hay Noche" as our MVP.

Currently, I am enrolled in Holberton's VR/AR specialization to expand my knowledge of game development. I am eager to continue exploring all the different facets of the tech industry where art and technology intersect.
